02 / About

Behind the Lens

Red Point Photograph is the work of Isaac Shinkle — photographer, climber, and desert wanderer based in La Verkin, Utah, minutes from Zion National Park.

In climbing, a redpoint is the clean send of a route you’ve worked for — every move earned. That’s the approach here: earn the light, earn the moment, press the shutter once it’s real.

The work centers on sexy, confident photoshoots with female models in wild Southern Utah locations. Sessions move from glamour and editorial beauty to artful nude and implied nude — often in golden-hour light pouring over red rock.

Trade for Prints

Most model sessions are done trade-for-prints — you get a session and digital gallery, I get portfolio work, and you order the prints you love. No big invoice, no pressure.

Custom, commercial, and concept shoots are quoted individually. Just ask.
